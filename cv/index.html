Professional Summary

Computational biologist working on epithelial plasticity, cancer initiation, and single-cell multi-omics. My research focuses on how oncogenic, mechanical, inflammatory, and microenvironmental signals reshape cell states and gene regulatory networks in glandular epithelia and skin.
